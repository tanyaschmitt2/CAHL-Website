About Our Company

Caribbean Atlantic Holdings Ghana Ltd. (CAHL) was established in 2004 following an invitation to President Kufour's inauguration, with a mission to support Ghana's industrial development by engaging the diaspora.

CAHL has extensive experience in Ghana's gold and minerals industry and has represented firms in the oil and gas sector. The company played a key role in implementing accounting software for Sonagas, Equatorial Guinea's gas company, and managed a gold mine in Obuasi.

Under the leadership of CEO Salilan Gomez, CAHL aims to become West Africa's premier methanol producer, focusing on sustainability, community development, and operational excellence.
