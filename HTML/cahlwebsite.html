About Our Company

Caribbean Atlantic Holdings Ghana Ltd. (CAHL) was established in 2004 following an invitation to President Kufour's inauguration, with a mission to support Ghana's industrial development by engaging the diaspora.

CAHL has extensive experience in Ghana's gold and minerals industry and has represented firms in the oil and gas sector. The company played a key role in implementing accounting software for Sonagas, Equatorial Guinea's gas company, and managed a gold mine in Obuasi.

Under the leadership of CEO Salilan Gomez, CAHL aims to become West Africa's premier methanol producer, focusing on sustainability, community development, and operational excellence.

Methanol Production Plant

West Africa's Premier Methanol Production Facility

About the Project

Our state-of-the-art methanol production plant represents a significant step forward in West Africa's industrial capabilities. Utilizing cutting-edge catalytic processes, we prioritize eco-friendly practices and sustainable development.

Key Features

  • Annual production capacity of 1,600,000 tons
  • Utilizes natural gas from the Ghana Gas Processing facility
  • Advanced catalytic conversion technology
  • Carbon capture and recycling systems
  • Zero wastewater discharge design

Economic Impact

Efficient local production facilities will lower import costs, enhance self-sufficiency, and stimulate economic growth in Ghana and West Africa. The plant is projected to create over 1,900 direct and indirect jobs, and significantly reduce regional reliance on methanol imports.
